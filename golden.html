Golden Triangle


Carrying within itself a spectacular history, spanning for thousands of years the destinations for Golden Triangle Tour introduce the best of olden days in India. This is a memorable highlight on any tour of India. The trio cities covered on India Golden Triangle Tour are the epitome of history, culture, and legacy. Starting with Delhi, the capital city for most of its history for the numerous classical kingdoms. Agra, with it being the seat of the mighty Mughal Empire, is redolent with some of the most astonishing monuments reminiscing of the Golden Era, which once ruled over India. Few need the introduction to the majestic Taj Mahal, located as the bright spot of Golden Triangle, India. However, the setting changes completely upon moving to Jaipur, with picturesque views of beautiful forts and lake palaces mesmerizing the heart. Framed in the shape of a triangle, the Golden Triangle Tour packages introduce one to a wide spectrum of colors of India and cannot be afforded to miss. Taste the wilderness of India by clubbing your Golden Triangle Tour with Ranthambore. Or, if you want to witness the diversified culture of India at one go, plan your Golden Triangle tour with Varanasi.
