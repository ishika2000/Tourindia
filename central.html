Central India


The very heart of India, Central India is a land of diversity. Blessed with alluring green environs, ferocious wildlife, the heritage of ancient temples, tribals who live in symphony with nature and other mystical pleasures, the central region of India spellbinds visitors. Central India tours comprise of the delights of Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh. The ancient Temples of Khajuraho, the wilderness of Bandhavgarh National Park, the archaeological sites in Chhattisgarh, the divine sources at Ujjain and Sarnath, the rugged forts at Gwalior and remnants of bygone glories at Orchha are enough to take you on an exciting exploration.
