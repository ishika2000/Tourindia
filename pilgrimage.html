Pilgrimage


This wonderful country called India is known to be the hub of religious and spiritual tourism. It is inhabited by people of several religious faiths. The country, thus houses various places of worship such as temples, mosques, gurudwaras, churches, mosques etc. A visit to these pilgrimage sites of India is truly a divine experience. Tourism of India has carefully crafted pilgrimage tours in India to Haridwar, Jyotirlinga destinations, Amarnath, Kailash Mansarovar, Mathura, Char Dham destinations, Buddhist destinations, Vaishno Devi, Shirdi, Sikh destinations and several other religious destinations so that you can seek the blessings of your lord and pay homage. You can pick a site as per your religious faith or can pick a destination related to another religion for getting a sneak-peak into their faiths and beliefs. The options are unending and include Dalai Lama’s home-in-exile- Dharamshala, the revered Sikh City- Amritsar, World’s Yoga Capital- Rishikesh, Hindu’s most revered Char Dham, one of India’s oldest city- Varanasi, the abode of Lord Shiva- Kailash Mansarovar, the stunning temples of Tamil Nadu and many more.
